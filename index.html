Know Who we are...

Indian Grocery Industries Limited has prevailed as the radical catalyst to serve and evolve the Indian retailers' community. The rate of increase of Unorganised retailers is soaring high and has reached a stressful amount of 1.1 crores. They withstand heavy baggage of not being acknowledged in the market and suffer tremendously to manage revenue and finance. Still, under IGIL, any unorganized retailer can enroll his business to gain links and have a full area of exposure to the market. IGIL works by the vision and cooperation of all retailers, suppliers, manufacturers, consumers, and investors.There is no exaggeration in the fact that IGIL is a one-way solution of all the difficulties that revolve around retailing. We sign an agreement with underprivileged retailers and provide them numerous facilities like advertising their store, giving transportation facilities for their products. We also modernize their stores through technology and provide a broader and greater market exposure. We even stand with them in the financial crisis and help them with all we can provide. By doing so and sailing the unorganized retailers to the shore of being organized, we play our part to level up the GDP. IGIL's vision is to continue the flux in the local community and to take it upto greater heights and, at the same time, to make every individual involved in this ecosystem to flourish and nurture.

Our Key Values

the IGIL community is based on an ecosystem in which each part of the work is handled well and is managed by step. This is done to avoid any mishappening that might occur while the process. To ensure proper functionality and customizability, we have come up with a foolproof formula in which each step is Monitored to perfection and then carried forward to the next step.

In this well-equipped cycle of our work, the first turn begins with the suppliers who supply the products, it then reaches to the retailers, where all products are handed over to them. Next up the product supply chain is carried in which products are made available to all the stores which have been registered under our community. This is done to make them well equipped with products and to let their stores expand.

Next up the products end in the hands of the customers, where it was made to be. Further on among these customers, we have our investors who contribute to the ecosystem through a monetary basis.

This never-ending cycle is made to acquire trust among our customers to ensure proper functioning. The efficiency of this shall soon be recognized globally.
